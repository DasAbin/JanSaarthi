=== SUMMARY ===
The railway department needs to buy about 7.5 acres of land in G. Medapadu village, Kakinada District, Andhra Pradesh. This land is for building a new bridge over the railway tracks. If your land is part of this project, you have 30 days from January 22, 2026, to object.

=== SIMPLE EXPLANATION ===
Imagine there's a railway crossing in your village where everyone waits for trains. The government wants to build a new road bridge high above the railway tracks, so no one has to stop anymore, making it safer and faster. To build this big bridge, they need some land. If your family owns a piece of land that is needed for this bridge, this paper tells you about it. You have about one month (30 days from January 22, 2026) to tell the main officer in Peddapuram if you don't want them to take your land. You can even go and talk to him about why you don't agree.

=== KEY POINTS ===
1. The railway department plans to acquire land in G. Medapadu village (Samalkot Mandal, Kakinada District, Andhra Pradesh).
2. The land is needed to build a new road bridge over the railway tracks, replacing the old crossing number 422.
3. A total of about 7.588 acres of land will be taken for this project.
4. If your land is listed in this document, you have 30 days from January 22, 2026, to formally object.
5. All objections must be sent in writing to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Peddapuram, who will listen to your concerns.

=== STEPS TO TAKE ===
1. **Check the list:** Look at the names and land details in the document to see if your land is mentioned.
2. **Get more information (optional but helpful):** You can visit the Revenue Divisional Officer (CALA) office in Peddapuram to see detailed maps and exact information about the affected land.
3. **Write your objection:** If your land is on the list and you want to disagree, write a letter to the Revenue Divisional Officer, Peddapuram. Clearly explain why you are objecting to your land being taken.
4. **Submit on time:** Make sure your written objection reaches the Revenue Divisional Officer, Peddapuram, within 30 days from January 22, 2026.
5. **Attend the meeting:** The officer will arrange a meeting where you can explain your objections in person, or you can ask a lawyer to speak for you.
