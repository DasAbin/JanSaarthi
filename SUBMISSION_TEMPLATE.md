## Project Summary

JanSaarthi is a voice-first, low-bandwidth civic access platform that helps citizens:
- Understand complex government documents (OCR + simplification)
- Discover and compare schemes and check eligibility (YojanaMatch)
- Get step-by-step form filling help (Form Helper)
- Learn essential civic/financial topics (micro-learning)

It is designed for inclusion and real-world impact, especially for users with limited literacy and connectivity.
